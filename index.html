About

Jessica Edwards is a Harvard senior in Pforzheimer House from Alexandria, Virginia studing Computer Science and Educational Studies. Her interests include advocating for diversity in tech, data science, machine learning, artificial intelligence, privacy, and education technology! 

Her other interests include serving in a Christian fellowship on campus, singing with the Kuumba Singers of Harvard College, and volunteering as a mentor and tutor. She's very excited for the opportunity expand her design and engineering skills through Intro to Digital Fabrication this semester!
